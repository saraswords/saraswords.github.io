About

I'm currently a post-bacc researcher at Ev Fedorenko's language lab at MIT. There I am the Project Coordinator of Interesting Brains, an endeavor which investigates the functional topography of cortex in the presence of substantial brain lesions (e.g., arachnoid cysts, resections, hydrocephalus).

I am originally from Memphis, Tennessee and enjoy reading and writing fiction in my free time. I also like a good beach or Great Lake (University of Michigan '20).
